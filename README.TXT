
ShieldsUp is a World of Warcraft user interface addon to provide textual
self-monitoring of shaman shield buffs.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
Features:

- Shows charge counters for Earth, Lightning, and/or Water Shield.
- Shows the current or last recipient of your Earth Shield.
- Alerts you when an active shield expires or is removed.
- Customizable colors, font, size, position, transparency, and more.
